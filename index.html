User Instructions

Large grids with big cell sizes can exceed browser limits (often around 8192×8192 or 16384×16384 pixels depending on the broswer) and consume huge amount of memory, causing blank canvases or crashes. If you have 1,000 cells each at 20×20 px, that’s 400 million pixels—too large for most systems. Keep total width and height under ~8k to avoid failures, find a balance between cell size and total cell count when necessary.

Default Settings

  • Grid Width: 30 columns
  • Grid Height: 30 rows
  • Cell Size: 10 pixels
  • Update Interval: 300ms
